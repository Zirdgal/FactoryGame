Welcome to Zird Factory Game!

Here you will learn how the game works:

First of all! Simple Gameplay Mechanics:

Honestly its quite simple, just click on the upgrade buttons when its green and you will overtime get points. Of course that is not all, SPS or Score Per Second is an acronym that helps you tell how much score you will get the next second. AP is for Ascension Points this is a secondary score you unlock after reaching 10 000 score for the first time!

Second, Ascension:

Before v.0.2.0 This mechanic was nothing more than a mere upgrade, but now it is itself the biggest part of this `game`. Ascension is the biggest part of your progression in ZFG(Zird Factory Game). It has it's own seperate upgrade tree with it's own currency. These upgrades are used for enahncing the regular upgrades and obtaining AP faster.
!!! WARNING !!!
Ascending will reset your regular upgrade progress aswell as your SPS.
